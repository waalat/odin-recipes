Baked Beans Recipe

Description

Baked beans is a dish traditionally containing white common beans that are parboiled and then baked in sauce at low temperature for a lengthy period.This recipe calls for 2 cans of baked beans with pork to be combined with sugar, molasses, onion, ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce and red wine vinegar in a baking dish. This mixture gets topped with two slices of bacon and popped into the oven for hour.

Ingredients:

  • 2 (15-ounce) cans baked beans with pork
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 2 slices bacon, cut in half

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the baked beans, brown sugar, molasses, onion, ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and red wine vinegar. Mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture into a baking dish and arrange the bacon slices on top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until the bacon is crispy and the beans are bubbly.
